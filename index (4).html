FAQ

What are the venue dimensions and ceiling height?

B1F totals 813 ping with 742.7 ping usable (8.6% common area), 4.5m ceiling height, and a U-shaped floor plate 44.17m deep × 51.13m wide. This scale is exceptionally rare in central Kaohsiung — ideal for KTV, nightclub, lounge bar, live house, large dining, banquet hall, and entertainment club flagships.

What is the zoning and are there business restrictions?

The property combines G2 office and B2 commercial zoning. Permitted uses include department store, supermarket, mass retail, retail market, wholesale, storefront, dining, KTV, comic/novel rental, nightlife economy, and disability employment service venues — exceptional business flexibility.

Where is it and what customer base surrounds it?

B1F of Chunghwa Trade Building, No. 91 Zhongshan 2nd Rd, Qianzhen District. Adjacent to FE21 Mega, Shin Kong Mitsukoshi, SOGO Sanduo Shopping District, and MRT Red Line Sanduo Station / Central Park Station. Hotel Nikko (260 rooms), Amnis (147 rooms), InterContinental (253 rooms), and Hanns House (540 rooms) collectively provide over 1,100 nightly rooms of dependable customer flow.

Will the underground location affect business?

For entertainment and leisure operations, underground placement is an advantage: year-round operation unaffected by weather; lower equipment maintenance cost; the large U-shaped plate allows easy zoning and private-room layout; independent glass-walled entry corridor provides the privacy and separation entertainment venues require.

What landmarks and major developments are nearby?

Adjacent to FE21 Mega Department Store (Kaohsiung's 10-time price leader), Shin Kong Mitsukoshi Sanduo, MRT Sanduo Shopping District Station (2-min walk), Dream Mall, National Kaohsiung Center for the Arts (Weiwuying), Kaohsiung Public Library Main Branch, Kaohsiung Music Center, Kaohsiung Exhibition Center, MLD Reading Plaza. Complete food, retail, hospitality, education, and entertainment infrastructure.

Is existing equipment included?

The building retains complete HVAC main units, M&E switchgear rooms, public restrooms, fire safety equipment, and other base infrastructure — significantly reducing engineering investment and lead time for incoming operators. Detailed equipment list available upon site visit.

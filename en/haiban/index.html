FAQ

What is the zoning and what businesses can operate here?

Zoned G3, the property may operate as a restaurant, aesthetic clinic, or office (including employee rest area and storage). The land is in a Commercial District, allowing wide flexibility — banking, café, coworking, home & living, and international supermarket are all viable use cases.

What is the total area and usable area?

Total floor area 623.08 ping; usable area 551.57 ping; ultra-low 11% common area ratio. Layout: B1F approx. 145 ping, 1F approx. 97 ping, 2F approx. 297 ping. Site area 1,580㎡ qualifies for all design-incentive thresholds.

How wide is the road frontage? Is outdoor signage feasible?

2F frontage is 34.5m and 1F frontage is 23.26m — the only sea-facing 34m-wide flagship in all of Kaohsiung. The 2F exterior wall is suitable for large outdoor signage, with strong landmark presence and LRT passing directly in front.

What major facilities are nearby?

Directly opposite the Asia New Bay Area: Kaohsiung Music Center, Exhibition Center, Port Cruise Terminal, Focus 13 mall, Hanshin Department Store, LRT C10 Glory Pier Station, Asia Yacht Marina, Coral Zone, Dolphin Walkway, Whale Promenade. Four major five-star hotels nearby (Nikko, Amnis, InterContinental, Hanns House) provide over 1,100 guest rooms — a stable source of business and leisure customers.

Does the Asia Asset Management Center policy apply?

Yes — the property is within the Asia Asset Management Center (Kaohsiung Zone). Five policy benefits apply: regulatory easing, cross-border financial services, diversified products, fund flexibility, and asset management partnership programs. Highly attractive to banks, insurance, brokerages, business centers, asset management firms, startups, and legal/tax practices.

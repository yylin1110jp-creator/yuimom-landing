How wide is the frontage? Triple-frontage corner?

Yes — the property sits on a corner with frontage on Baotai Rd, Sanshang St, and Shanhe St. Baotai Rd frontage 23.38m, Sanshang St frontage 18.43m. As a true corner parcel with super-sized frontage, it's the prime choice for flagship corporate identity.
